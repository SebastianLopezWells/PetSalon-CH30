Daycare Facility: Where does my dog play while I'm away?

    We have 3 outdoor and 3 indoor play areas for (y)our pets. All pets are mixed by temperament. We rotate them throughout the day to make sure they get a chance to nap and relax as much as they run and play. Our turfed outdoor play area has canopies, water toys, misters and plenty of water bowls to keep our doggies cool on hot days. We also have 3 play yards in our cool basement level with individual drains and air to keep everyone clean and happy. All yards are supervised by our Pack Leaders to ensure that everyone is having fun and playing safe. We offer a variety of packages to fit your lifestyle.

What is a Temperament Test?

    All of our four legged friends are required to go through a series of tests (2-4 hours in duration) under the watchful eye of our Pack Leaders and trainers in order to attend daycare or boarding. During each test, we will be measuring the dog's reaction to various elements such as, sound, touch, leash handling, separation and play with humans and other dogs. Each dog will be categorized as “aggressive”, “stable”, or “confident”. Upon passing, your dog is ready to participate in any of our dog services.

What is required for a temperament?
